Privacy policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

Who I am

Focus4ward is the independent practice of Miri Blum, a fractional CMO based in Paris. I am the data controller for this website. You can reach me any time at miri@focus4ward.co.

What I collect

I only collect what I need to talk with you and to keep the site working.

  • Contact form. When you use the form on the contact page, I collect your name, company, email, phone number (optional), your role, your message, and how you heard about me. Submissions are processed and stored through Netlify Forms.
  • Analytics. This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use it. It sets cookies and collects anonymised usage data such as pages viewed, approximate location, and device type.

Why I collect it

Two reasons. To respond to your enquiry and explore working together, which are steps taken at your request before any contract. And to understand and improve the site, which is my legitimate interest in running it well.

Who has access

Your data is handled by me and by the services that run this site: Netlify for form submissions and Google for analytics. I never sell your data, and I never share it for third-party marketing.

How long I keep it

Contact submissions are kept only as long as needed to respond and, if we end up working together, for the duration of our relationship plus any period the law requires. Analytics data follows Google Analytics retention settings.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you can ask to access, correct, delete, or restrict your data, or object to how it is processed. Email miri@focus4ward.co and I will respond within one month. If you are in France, you can also lodge a complaint with the CNIL.

Cookies

This site uses analytics cookies only. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings, and the site still works without them.

Changes to this policy

I may update this policy as the site evolves. The date at the top always shows the latest revision.
